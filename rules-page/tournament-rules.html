Winter Pokémon Tournament Rules

  1. ❄️ Every Trainer must arrive with a smile. Grumpy Trainers may receive a friendly "Frost Warning."
  2. 🎒 Bring your own device and make sure it's fully charged before the tournament begins.
  3. 🧊 Ice-type Pokémon are not required, but they are highly encouraged to embrace the winter spirit.
  4. ☃️ Snowballs are for decoration only. Throwing them at judges or other Trainers is strictly prohibited.
  5. 🤝 Respect your opponents, judges, and spectators at all times. Good sportsmanship is worth more than any badge.
  6. ⚡ Pikachu hugs are optional but always appreciated.
  7. 🍫 Hot chocolate breaks may occur at any moment if the weather becomes too chilly.
  8. 🐧 Trainers dressed in winter-themed outfits earn maximum style points (unfortunately, style points do not affect tournament results).
  9. 🎁 The Tournament Director reserves the right to award surprise prizes for the funniest costume, best sportsmanship, or most dramatic victory celebration.
  10. ❄️ If you encounter a wild Snorlax blocking the entrance, please wait patiently or offer it a snack.
  11. 🏆 Winning is great, but making new friends is the real legendary achievement.
  12. 🌨️ Above all else, have fun and enjoy the Winter Pokémon Tournament!

Special Notice: Any sightings of Articuno, Delibird, or Alolan Vulpix must be reported immediately to the Tournament Staff. Rewards are not guaranteed, but bragging rights certainly are. ❄️
